The Rationale

Why these strategies — and why now

AI commoditized data collection in 2024. The new scarcity is synthesis and action. Here's how SlyBri is built to win.

Market Timing

AI commoditized data collection in 2024. The new scarcity is synthesis and action. SlyBri enters as costs to build a data pipeline hit near-zero — but buyer trust in raw dashboards is at a low.

Competitive Positioning

Enterprise tools (Crayon, Klue, Bombora) are bloated and expensive. Lightweight point-tools lack depth. SlyBri occupies the credible middle: serious depth, startup speed, mid-market price.

Monetisation Logic

Seat-based SaaS with a usage floor. Upsell on integrations (Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot), custom competitor watchlists, and a futures tier for board-level narrative generation.

Defensibility

The moat isn't the AI — it's the proprietary signal corpus and the action layer trained on tens of thousands of real competitive decisions made by SlyBri users over time.
